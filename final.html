when I see ugly CSS online,

Where the box model just won't quite align.

I wonder why not;

Were they never taught

To use a grid-based CSS design?

Semantic design goes largely unseen,

Unless you are a web crawling machine

But semantics aid

the SEO trade

they add meaning (if you know what I mean)

Responsive design adjusts sizes

For mobile and odd shaped devices

to optimize the fit

shape your content a bit;

and state your case with few compromises.
