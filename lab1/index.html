Objective Statement

The purpose of my resume is to show people that my accomplishments and leadership as Queen of England will help me be successful if I were to be queen of Carlesonia. I have been Queen of England since I was 25 years of age so I most definitely have experience in the position of a queen. I wish to prove to people that if chosen, I will be the best fit as ruler of Carlesonia. I want someone who doesn't know a thing about me to finish reading my resume and be able to list all of my accomplishments in my 44 years of ruling the throne.

Titles and Honours

My formal title is: Her Majesty Elizabeth, by the Grace of God, of England, Princess of York, Duchess of Edinburgh, Countess of Merioneth, Baroness Greenwich, Sovereign of the Most Honourable Order of the Garter, Sovereign of the Most Honourable Order of the Bath, Sovereign of the Most Ancient and Most Noble Order of the Thistle, Sovereign of the Most Illustrious Order of Saint Patrick, Sovereign of the Most Distinguished Order of Saint Michael and Saint George, and Defender of the Faith.

Endorsements
